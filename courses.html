// what are freestyle courses?

Standalone courses. Real skills. No long commitment.

A freestyle course is a standalone program built around a single, high-demand skill. You come in, learn it properly, and leave with something real to show for it - and you can upgrade into a full career track any time.

01

Flexible Duration

From 4-week intensive sprints to 6-month deep dives - every course is timed to match the depth of the skill, not padded to fill a calendar.

02

Upgrade Anytime

Loved your course? Step up into a BBT career track and unlock the full 4-phase Learning Ecosystem - Trainee, Intern, Shadow, Expert.

03

Industry-Recognized Certificate

Every course ends with a BBT completion certificate that reflects the skills you've actually built - not just the hours you sat through.

// questions we get asked all the time

Straight answers.

What's the difference between a freestyle course and a career track?
A freestyle course is a standalone program focused on one specific skill. A career track is a full structured pathway from beginner to industry-ready professional. You can start with a course and upgrade to a track at any time.
Can I join with absolutely no prior experience?
Yes. Most BBT courses are built for complete beginners.
Are the courses available online?
Yes. All courses are available onsite at our Lahore campus, online via live sessions, or in a hybrid format.
Do I get a certificate after completing a course?
Yes - every BBT course ends with an industry-recognized completion certificate that reflects the skills you've built.
Can I switch to a career track after a freestyle course?
Absolutely. Your course progress is recognized when you join a track - you don't start from zero, you continue from where your skill level already is.
How long are the courses?
Durations range from 2 to 6 months, depending on the depth of the subject.
Where is BBT located?
Big Binary Trainings is located at 444-Q, DHA Phase II, Lahore. Online students can attend from anywhere.
