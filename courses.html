// the path, in detail

How each phase carries into the next.

Whichever track you pick, you move through the same four phases. The duration shifts by discipline; the structure doesn't.

PHASE_01 · 1–3 MONTHS

Trainee - build the foundations.

Onsite + Online
// what you do

Practical, hands-on IT training. Tools, syntax, workflows, fundamentals you'll reach for every day after.

// outcome

A working foundation in your discipline + a small portfolio of warm-up projects on GitHub.

// carries into

Phase 02. You'll be ready to join a real project team on day one.

PHASE_02 · 2–4 MONTHS

Internee - ship real projects.

Software house
// what you do

Internship inside our software-house network. Real briefs, real deadlines, real code review.

// outcome

Shipped features in a production project + a written reference from your team lead.

// carries into

Phase 03. Now you've shipped - next you watch how senior people decide what to ship.

PHASE_03 · 1–2 MONTHS

Shadow - learn beside mentors.

Mentor 1:1
// what you do

Paired with a practising senior in your discipline. Sit in on planning, reviews, postmortems.

// outcome

A working mental model of how real teams trade off scope, quality, speed and cost.

// carries into

Phase 04. Time to take ownership of something end-to-end.

PHASE_04 · 2–3 MONTHS

Expert - become industry-ready.

Capstone
// what you do

Lead a capstone project end-to-end. Specialise. Deepen. Write, present, defend the work.

// outcome

A portfolio piece + verifiable BBT credential + introductions inside our software-house network.

// carries into

Your first role - full-time, contract or freelance. The career, not the certificate.
