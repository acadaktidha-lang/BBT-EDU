// what you will learn

Animation - from the ground up.

Not a little of everything - enough of everything to build something real, end to end, on your own.

// core skills
  • ▪Principles of Animation - Deep dive into the 12 essential rules of animation, including squash and stretch, anticipation, and follow-through.
  • ▪Character Design & Rigging - Learn to create memorable characters and build digital skeletons for fluid, lifelike movement.
  • ▪Storyboarding & Layout - Master the art of visual storytelling to plan compelling scenes that keep your audience engaged.
// going further
  • ▪Motion Graphics - Understand how to use typography and shape manipulation for high-impact commercial and marketing projects.
  • ▪Advanced Rendering - Discover how to light, texture, and render your scenes for a professional, cinematic finish.
