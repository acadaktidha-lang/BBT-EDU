// what is navttc

The national authority behind Pakistan's skilled workforce.

The National Vocational & Technical Training Commission (NAVTTC) is the apex government body that regulates, develops and certifies technical and vocational education across Pakistan. It sets the national skills standards that employers - at home and abroad - rely on when hiring.

As a NAVTTC-accredited institute, Big Binary Trainings delivers approved curricula and issues certification that carries the weight of the federal government, opening doors to local jobs, freelancing income and international employment.

Government accreditation

Curriculum and certification approved and regulated by a federal commission - not a private certificate.

Internationally recognized

NAVTTC certification is accepted by employers abroad, including the Gulf and Saudi labour markets.

Free, merit-based access

Training is fully funded for eligible candidates - skills development without a financial barrier.

// key functions

What NAVTTC does for the country.

Sets national skill standards

Defines competency standards and curricula so every certified skill means the same thing nationwide.

Accredits training institutes

Registers and audits institutes like BBT so learners train only at quality-assured centres.

Certifies skilled workers

Issues nationally recognized certificates that verify a worker's competence to employers.

Enables overseas employment

Aligns Pakistani skills with international demand to place workers in foreign labour markets.

Drives industry-led training

Works with industry to keep curricula matched to the skills employers actually need today.

Funds free skill programs

Runs fully funded national programs so youth can train in high-demand skills at no cost.

Promotes emerging technologies

Introduces AI, cloud and other "skills of tomorrow" into the national training mainstream.

// frequently asked questions

NAVTTC, answered.

Are NAVTTC courses really free?
Yes. For eligible applicants, NAVTTC courses at BBT are fully funded under government skills programs - there is no tuition fee for the training or the certificate.
Who is eligible to apply?
NAVTTC programs are aimed at Pakistani youth looking to build market-relevant digital skills. Exact age and education criteria vary by program - submit an application and our team will confirm your eligibility.
Is the certificate recognized internationally?
NAVTTC certification is a government-issued, nationally recognized credential that is also accepted by many overseas employers - a real advantage for those seeking work in the Gulf and other international markets.
Do I need prior experience?
No. These courses are designed to take beginners to job-ready - they start from fundamentals and build up through hands-on, project-based learning, so motivated newcomers are welcome.
Are classes online or on-campus?
BBT runs both on-campus sessions at our Lahore DHA campus and online cohorts - same curriculum, same mentors and the same NAVTTC certification, so you can train in the format that suits you.
