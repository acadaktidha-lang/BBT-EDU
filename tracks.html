// quick questions - straight answers

Everything you need to know before joining.

Do I need prior coding experience?
No. Most tracks start from the basics - UI/UX, Marketing and Full Stack have fully beginner-friendly paths. For advanced tracks like Agentic AI we recommend basic Python, and we can help you get there.
Is the internship paid?
Yes. Every student gets a paid internship during Phase 2 (Intern) and Phase 3 (Shadow). You earn while you learn.
What is the fee structure?
Fees vary by track and duration. We offer 0% EMI through partner banks, merit scholarships up to 40%, and flexible payment plans. Chat with an advisor for exact details.
Do you offer job placement after the course?
Yes. Our placement rate is 91% within 6 months of graduation. We provide resume prep, mock interviews and direct referrals to our software-house partners.
Is this online or onsite?
Both. Onsite training at our DHA Phase 2, Lahore campus, and online live classes for students anywhere in Pakistan - same curriculum, same mentors, same certification.
What certification will I get?
A BBT course-completion certificate, NAVTTC-accredited certification and PITB-recognized training proof - all verifiable online so employers can check instantly.
Can I switch tracks after starting?
Yes - within the first 2 weeks. Talk to your mentor and we'll help you find the right fit.
