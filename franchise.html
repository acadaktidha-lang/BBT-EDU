REVENUE STREAMS

MULTIPLE WAYS TO EARN.

BBT franchises generate revenue from more than one direction. The diversity of income sources is part of what makes the model resilient.

PRIMARY

COURSE FEES

Regular student enrolments across all three schools. Fee structures set per course, per cohort. Your share after BBT royalty is your core monthly income.

GOVERNMENT

NAVTTC + INSTITUTIONAL PROJECTS

Federal and provincial government-funded IT training programmes. BBT accesses these contracts centrally and routes them through franchise units.

CORPORATE

CUSTOMISED CORPORATE TRAINING

B2B training packages for companies, government agencies, and school education boards. Higher per-batch value than individual enrolments.

SOFTWARE

SOFTWARE HOUSE JV / PARTNERSHIPS

Selected franchise locations can participate in BBT's software house joint venture model - extending revenue beyond education into service delivery.

// franchise faq

The questions we get most.

Q_01What's the total ballpark to open a Standard campus?
Franchise fee from PKR 5M, plus fit-out (PKR 4–8M depending on city & size), plus 12 months of working capital. We share a full city-specific model during the discovery call.
Q_02How exclusive is the territory?
Fully exclusive operating rights within a defined area (city or zone of a metro) for the contract term. No second BBT in your patch.
Q_03Do I need to come from an education background?
No. We've shortlisted business operators, fmcg distributors, returning expats, doctors and software engineers. What matters is operator discipline and capital, not prior teaching experience.
Q_04Who hires and pays the mentors?
You hire and employ them; HQ certifies them. We help with sourcing, screening and the certification programme. Standard markets are mostly self-staffed by month 3.
Q_05What happens if a partner underperforms?
Quarterly business reviews catch issues early. If a campus persistently misses quality or growth thresholds, we intervene with on-site support before any harder action.
Q_06Are international partners welcome?
Yes - GCC and select South Asian markets are open. International terms differ slightly to account for currency & compliance; we walk you through them at discovery.
Q_07Can I franchise just the freestyle side?
Yes - the Starter format is exactly this. Freestyle courses + 2 tracks, smaller footprint, faster ramp. Good entry point for satellite cities.
